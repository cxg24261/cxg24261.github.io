Being Musicians In Athens

In around August of 2019, Retrophile made its debut as a band. With lead singer/guitarist Cristian Gutierrez and guitarist Zach Watkins having been in a band previously named "RuHaa!", the two had already had some experience in and around the Athens music scene.

Retrophile has been actively performing at venues and for some organizations at UGA and are in the process of releasing new music. Cristian Gutierrez, says Zach and I have always enjoyed playing music and we've wanted to show our sound to everyone we know. So this new band gives us the opportunity to show the world what we got.

In a typical week, the members of Retrophile try and fit in school, band practice, work, and gigs altogether.
